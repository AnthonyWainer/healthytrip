5 simple steps

1

Fill out our form and we’ll diagnose you

The first step is to fill out our form and send us your jaw X-ray. It must be no more than 3 months old. With this information, our specialists will diagnose you and write a detailed, personalized treatment. Soon after, you will be hearing from us. We will send you 4 quotes of our best dental specialists. You will then have the option to talk live to two of the specialist you have chosen from the quotes we send you. Now you are good to go. After you choose and buy our services, you could:

Book your trip yourself. For more information check our Guide to Travel to Peru, in which you will find useful information about hotels, tours and restaurants.

We book everything for you. Tell us what you need here.

2

Meeting in the airport and drive to hotel

We are waiting for you at the airport and go to the hotel, we exchange the money for the various needs and sign all documents at the hotel and give you time to relax, when you rested you can contact us and we can go to walk in Lima or somewhere to dine

3

Treat you teeth

At the specified time, we go to the clinic, where all procedures will be carried out, after which payment is made. You can spend an entertaining program on that day (if you like), but it will be better if you will rest.

4

Entertainment program

We make an entertainment program based on your wishes during the conversation. During your stay in Lima, your favorite rock band or an interesting show may take place, we will all take it into consideration, it is obligatory to have a tour of Lima and an assessment of the local cuisine. We will leave only good memories of Lima

5

Come home

We will go with you to airport and will wait your flight. Only after your landing on airplane we can be calm.
